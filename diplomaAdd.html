Centralized Admission Process (CAP) for post ssc diploma

Steps to be followed for Post SSC Diploma Addmission


  1. Purchase Application kit from ARC
  2. Online registration of application for admission through computer connected to Internet on the website for Maharashtra State/All India/J & K Migrant/Minority Candidates.
  3. Documents verification, updating and confirmation of Application Form for Admission in person at Facilitation Center(FC).
  4. Display of the provisional merit lists on website www.dtemaharashtra.gov.in.
  5. Submission of grievance, if any, at FC
  6. Display of the final merit lists for all types of candidates on website.
  7. Display of Category wise Seat Distribution (Seat Matrix) for CAP Round I
  8. Online Submission & Confirmation of Option Form for CAP Round-I,II,III through candidates Login by himself/herself through the website.
  9. Display of Provisional Allotment of CAP Round-I
  10. Reporting to the Admission Reporting Centre (ARC) for CAP Round I. (If seat is allotted, Report to ARC, Select Freeze/Slide/Float option. Produce Original Documents for Verification & Pay Seat Acceptance Fee in the form DD.)
  11. Display of Provisional Allotment of CAP Round-II
  12. Reporting to the Admission Reporting Centre (ARC) for CAP Round II. (If seat is allotted first time i.e. in Round II , Report to ARC, Select Freeze/Slide/Float option. Produce Original Documents for Verification & Pay Seat Acceptance Fee in the form DD.)
  13. Display of Provisional Allotment of CAP Round-III
  14. Reporting to the Admission Reporting Centre (ARC) for CAP Round III. (If seat is allotted first time i.e. in Round III , Report to ARC, Select Freeze/Slide/Float option. Produce Original Documents for Verification & Pay Seat Acceptance Fee in the form DD.)
  15. Display of Vacant Seats for CAP Round-IV
  16. Online Submission & Confirmation of fresh Option Form for CAP Round-IV through candidates Login by himself/herself through the website. For all candidates who have;
    • Not been allotted any seat in Rounds I/II/III
    • Not participated by the way of not filling the Option form in CAP Round I
    • Those who have taken and cancelled their admission in the earlier rounds
    • Those who have Freezed the admission in earlier rounds but now are desirous of changing the option to Floating or Sliding
  17. Display of Provisional Allotment of CAP Round-IV
  18. Reporting to the ARC as per Allotment of CAP Round IV
    19. (If seat is allotted first time i.e. in Round IV, Report to ARC, Produce Original Documents for Verification & Pay Seat Acceptance Fee in the form DD.)
  19. For all candidates: Reporting to the Allotted Institute and Confirmation of Admission by submitting required documents and paying remaining fees, as per the Allotment.
